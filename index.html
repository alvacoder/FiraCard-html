How FiraCard works

Send a group card in 3 easy steps (easy, peasy, lemon squeezy)

1 Create a board

Click on a blank board and personalise it. Choose your preferred background and add details about the celebrant

2 Invite contributors (or not)

Share your board with contributors so they can add their wishes too. They can add messages, photos, GIFs & videos.

3 Send to the special person

Click send to deliver all your wishes in one group card

What makes Firacard so cool

We have some cool features that will make you and your friends happy

As many boards as you want, free & YOURS

You have more than one special person, we do too and so we have made sure that you can create as many boards as you like for free. You can add images, messages and videos to all of your boards.

15 Contributors go FREE

Lots of things are more fun when we do them together; so you can invite 15 people for free to help you make your group card memorable. With Firacard,everyone can join in.

Customisable & Yours FOREVER

You can personalise your board with a choice of backgrounds, colours, and fonts to tailor it for your special person or simply to show your creativity. What’s more, you can download and keep your firacard of pictures, videos and messages forever. A card and a keep-sake all in one. Versatile!

We are easy to use

Our step-by-step guide means anyone can create a group card or contribute to an existing one. You can give something amazing to someone special easily and without stress.
