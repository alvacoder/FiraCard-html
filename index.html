How FiraCard works

Create a group board for your friend. Add messages, photos, gifs and more and let multiple contributors add remotely to create a thoughtful collection to be shared with your special friend.

1 Create a board

Simply sign-up, select to create a board and you will be able to select from a variety of options to tailor your board for the occasion. Find the perfect one for your special person.

2 Invite contributors(or not)

Your board might be just from you to someone special but you can also invite others to add their messages to the board to make your family, friends and loved ones feel even more loved. Just add in their email addresses and they will receive a link to add their message. You will also be able to send contributors a link, making it easy to share over social media channels.

3 Send your wishes

Once everyone has added their messages to the board which may be text, photos, GIFs or videos, then you are ready to send! You will be able to send the board through a link or via an email through FiraCard to the recipient. Not only will the recipient receive a beautiful board online, they will also be able to download any images, videos or messages and keep those forever.

Exclusive Features

We have some cool features that will make your special friend happy

Have as many people contribute as needed

You can send a board just from you to your special person. Or you can ask other people to contribute as well by simply entering their email to end a link. Maybe you want everyone in the office to wish someone a congratulations or maybe it’s a family member's big birthday. With Firacard, everyone gets to join in.

Create as many boards as you want, absolutely free

You can create as many boards as you like for free and include up to 15 contributors. If you need more contributors than this, follow our simple pricing plan to learn more.

We are easy to use

Our step-by-step guide means anyone will be able to create their first board or contribute to an existing one. We want everyone to be able to use Firacard and give something amazing to their loved ones.

Customisable and keepable

Personalise your board with a choice of templates, colours and gifs to tailor for you special person. What’s more, pictures, videos and messages can be downloaded from the board and kept forever. A card and a keep-sake all in one.
